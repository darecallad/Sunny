Our Staff

Sunny Child Care provides a comprehensive early childhood program catering to children from infancy to Kindergarten-entry, staffed by highly qualified early educators. Our curriculum and philosophical framework are rooted in play-based and research-based approaches.

Each of our Early Education teachers meets the following requirements:

  • - All SCC Early Educators undergo fingerprinting and background clearance.
  • - They hold CPR/First Aid certification.
  • - Our teachers are fully qualified, exceeding the minimum state requirements in terms of their educational backgrounds.

    Our commitment to maintaining low teacher-to-student ratios enables our educators to concentrate on the unique learning styles and requirements of each child within our center. We firmly believe that teachers play a pivotal role in a child's early development, and we organize biannual teacher training sessions to ensure our staff remains highly skilled, well-prepared, and dedicated to delivering the best possible care to children. Their unwavering dedication is focused on providing the optimal experience for the children entrusted to their care.
