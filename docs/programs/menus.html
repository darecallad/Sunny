Menu

Our meal plans for children are carefully crafted to meet their unique nutritional requirements and support their growth and development. These meal plans are thoughtfully designed to provide a balanced and wholesome diet that includes a variety of food groups, ensuring that children receive the essential nutrients they need for optimal health.

Typically, meal plans for children include a combination of the following:

  • Protein:

    Sources of protein such as lean meats, poultry, fish, eggs, dairy products, and plant-based options like beans, lentils, and tofu are included to support muscle development and overall growth.

  • Fruits and Vegetables

    A wide variety of colorful fruits and vegetables are incorporated to provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber. These foods promote strong immune systems and overall well-being.

  • Whole Grains:

    Foods like whole-grain bread, pasta, rice, and cereals offer complex carbohydrates that supply energy for active children and aid in brain function.

  • Dairy or Dairy Alternatives:

    Milk, yogurt, and cheese, or dairy alternatives like fortified soy or almond milk, provide calcium for strong bones and teeth.

  • Healthy Fats:

    Foods containing healthy fats, such as avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil, contribute to brain development and overall health.

  • Hydration:

    Adequate water intake is crucial for children's health. Water is the primary beverage of choice, and limited servings of 100% fruit juices are also included in moderation.

  • Snacks:

    Nutrient-dense snacks like fresh fruit, yogurt, or whole-grain crackers are offered between meals to keep energy levels stable and prevent hunger.

  • Portion Sizes:

    Meals are portioned appropriately for a child's age and activity level to ensure they receive the right amount of calories and nutrients.

  • Variety:

    Meal plans for children aim to introduce a wide range of foods to encourage diverse tastes and preferences while providing a broad spectrum of nutrients.

  • Allergies and Dietary Restrictions:

    Special attention is given to accommodate any food allergies or dietary restrictions a child may have.

Incorporating these elements into a child's meal plan helps foster healthy eating habits from an early age and provides them with the nutrition they need to thrive. Please see our monthly menu plan.
