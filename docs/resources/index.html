Enrollment, Student Information and Communication Systems

At Sunny Child Care, we use the Brightwheel to connect our families. Brightwheel offers a comprehensive set of features to enhance your child's learning journey. You can effortlessly stay connected and engaged with real-time updates, including attendance records, school announcements, personal messages from teachers, daily insights, and mobile photos.

Our user-friendly digital check-in system ensures secure drop-offs and pick-ups, and you have the convenience of adding approved adults. Simplify payments with our paperless billing option, allowing for digital transactions directly from your bank account or credit/debit card, complete with email receipts. Additionally, Brightwheel fosters a sense of community by enabling you to invite grandparents, nannies, and friends, all while maintaining control over their access and involvement.
