Why Sunny Child Care

Bilingual

Our bilingual program offers young learners a language head start, cognitive skills, and global awareness. It prepares them for school and promotes inclusivity and parent engagement.

Environment

In our spacious childcare center, safety is paramount. Our large, child-friendly classrooms encourage growth and exploration while rigorous safety measures ensure your child's well-being.

Academics

Our childcare center embraces a play-based approach to early education, fostering life skills, social development, and STEAM elements naturally through hands-on experiences. We nurture well-rounded, academically proficient, and socially adept individuals.

Staff

Our childcare staff, with a low teacher-student ratio, is bilingual, well-trained, and passionate. We foster a warm, nurturing environment and prioritize ongoing professional development. Their diverse talents enrich the learning experience.

Nutrition

At our child care center, your child's well-being is our top priority. We provide nutritious warm lunches and dinners, fresh fruits, and organic milk in our monthly meal plans, ensuring their healthy development.

Communication

At Sunny Child Care, we use Brightwheel for real-time updates, a continuous feed of daily activities, and seamless parent-teacher communication. Simplify check-ins, access calendars, and streamline billing. Join us today!
