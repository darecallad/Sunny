Welcome to
Sunny Child Care

We are committed to provide families with a nurturing and enriching environment where the classrooms feel just like home. Each child’s world gets a little bigger every time they step, or crawl, or toll, or hop into our classroom. Our classrooms and curriculums are designed around their unique needs where children can achieve their maximum potential in the areas of their intellect, emotional well-being, social skills, physical fitness, spiritual growth and cultural needs in our Mandarin-English immersion program.
