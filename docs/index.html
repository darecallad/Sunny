Welcome to SUNNY

CHILD CARE

We are committed to provide families with a nurturing and enriching environment where the classrooms feel just like home. Each child’s world gets a little bigger every time they step, or crawl, or toll, or hop into our classroom. Our classrooms and curriculums are designed around their unique needs where children can achieve their maximum potential in the areas of their intellect, emotional well-being, social skills, physical fitness, spiritual growth and cultural needs in our Mandarin-English immersion program.

Why Suuny Child Care

Safety

Keeping our children safe is our top priority and we take it very seriously. Your local Building Kidz preschool is sanitized daily and comprehensive safety protocols have been implemented.

Learn More

Learning Through Life Experiences

Our proprietary LTLE curriculum complements dedicated and focused play areas to accelerate cognitive, emotional, and social development.

Learn More

Nutritious Meals

For campuses with kitchens, we prepare monthly meal plans and provide nutrient-dense choices daily. At our campuses without kitchens, we prepare nutritious snacks daily to supplement lunches provided from home.

Environment

Our children are continuously nurtured by exceptional teachers and caregivers along with age appropriate educational materials for kinesthetic, audial, visual, and touch learners.

Academics

I was also afraid because my son was 11 months he might have issues adjusting especially because of what happened at the home daycare. Both stefanie and elissa were AMAZING in helping my kid adjust.
